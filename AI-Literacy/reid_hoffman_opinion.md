English Perspective

**Reid Hoffman argues in "Blitzscaling":** In uncertain environments, speed trumps efficiency. He emphasizes that in an era of rapid technological and market changes, companies must prioritize speed over perfection.

Core principles of Blitzscaling:

  * Prioritize speed over efficiency
  * Make decisions under uncertainty
  * Accept "messiness" and imperfection
  * Focus on products that can generate network effects

**On AI:** Hoffman views AI as an amplifier of human capabilities, not a replacement. He emphasizes that AI tools should be used to enhance human creativity and decision-making abilities.
